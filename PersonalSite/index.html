About Me

I am an 18-year-old recent High School graduate. I love anything involved with computers, I've even built 3 of my own. I love coding and video games. My favorite thing is solving a puzzle in unique ways. I also love doing house renovations, something I frequently do with my dad who owns his own property investment company. You can usually find me playing video games in my free time though. That's what I do the most, right now my favorite game is Destiny 2. I have a dog who lives with my mom and my cat who lives with me, they are both a huge part of my life. Other than that there isn't much left of my life. It's pretty straightforward. Before Centriq, I was a Sacker at Price Chopper, a common grocery store in the Midwest. I wanted to get into IT/Web Development because I love technology. Over the last year of my High School career, I dove deep into coding, I wanted to learn everything about it. Finally, I decided instead of attending a 4-year college that I would attend Centriq and get right into the workforce. In my free time, I like to do renovation projects, help people with hardware/software issues on their computers, and play video games.

Driven

Every day I am driven to learn more. I want to consume knowledge. I love learning more and more about the online world and the languages that make it run.

Consistent

I have always been consistent with work. Everyday of my profressional career I have strived to be on time and ready to go. I pride myself on my speediness of getting work done.

Innovative

I am constantly thinking of new ways to streamline things, whethers its hundreds or thousands of lines of code or just how to get to work quicker, finding the best possible option is always something I am thinking about.

Critical
